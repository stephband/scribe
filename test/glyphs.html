Scribe Glyphs

From the SMuFL spec at w3c.github.io/smufl/latest/index.html, and its description of SMuFL font metrics.

Each glyph presented here in a span. Spans with classes have their line-height adjusted to fit their glyphs. By default SMuFL glyphs have enormous line boxes. I don't know why.

Scribe 0.5 Scribe is a new, modern way to display music on the web, packaged in a custom element you can drop into a web page. Fluid and responsive Scribe notation embraces the flow of the web, making it highly readable. It adapts to the constraints of your device with a fluidity that no other notation solution manages. (Several figures at different widths) Highly styleable Scribe is layed out in CSS using flex and grid, and sized relative to font-size. With a little CSS know-how it is highly customisable. (Some music multiplied onto a background image, compact) SMuFL compliant fonts Scribe employs standard SMuFL fonts to render musical symbols. It comes packaged and tested with 5 openly licensed SMuFL fonts out of the bag. (4 examples, several bars in each font) 6 Staves Comes with 6 stave types – treble, bass, piano, violin, drum and percussion staves. Plus, staves are extensible – it is possible to define your own.
